GRASSROOTSYOUNG BLASTERS

Kerala Blasters FC kick started the KBFC Young Blasters program, a Grassroots and Youth development initiative for budding and passionate talents across the state in November 2019 with the launch of their Parkway Football Centre. With an objective to spread the KBFC style of football across Kerala, the Young Blasters program will be completely organized and coordinated by Kerala Blasters FC.

As torchbearers of football development in Kerala, Technique, Purpose and Joy becomes the three core values that the club upholds through Young Blasters. A standardised, scientific and procedural form of coaching that paves way to a bright career in football, thereby achieving pure joy from the sport is the key idea behind this initiative.Young Blasters operate on three different segments: KBFC Young Blasters Football Schools. KBFC Young Blasters Affiliated Academy and KBFC Young Blasters Football Centres.In the first two categories, all Technical support including a KBFC certified coach and curriculum will be provided by the club to the partner schools and affiliated academies. Training ready grounds and enrolments will be managed by the schools and academies.
